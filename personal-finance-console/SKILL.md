## Regular Review Cadence

### Monthly (15 min)
- Update account balances
- Review spending vs. budget
- Check progress on active goals
- Address any alerts

### Quarterly (1 hour)
- Full net worth update
- Cash flow analysis
- Goal progress review
- Rebalance investments if needed
- Tax estimate update

### Annually (3 hours)
- Comprehensive financial review
- Set new year goals
- Tax planning meeting prep
- Insurance coverage review
- Update financial plan

## Data Input Requirements

To generate dashboards, provide:
1. **Account Balances**: All asset and liability accounts
2. **Income Sources**: Salary, bonuses, side income
3. **Expense Categories**: Monthly spending by category
4. **Goals**: Target amounts and timelines
5. **Tax Information**: Current withholding, estimated income

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Monthly Check-In
"Here are my updated account balances: [data]. Generate my monthly financial dashboard."

### Use Case 2: Goal Evaluation
"I want to save $XX,XXX for a rental property down payment in 2 years. Am I on track based on my current finances?"

### Use Case 3: Scenario Analysis
"Compare my finances if I: (A) Stay in current job, (B) Take new job with $XX,XXX higher salary but longer commute."

### Use Case 4: Tax Planning
"I'm expecting a $XX,XXX bonus in December. What's my tax exposure and how can I optimize?"

## Getting Started

To use this skill:
1. Share your current financial data (balances, income, expenses)
2. Define your financial goals and timelines
3. Indicate what view you need (net worth, cash flow, scenario, etc.)
4. I'll generate a clear, actionable dashboard with insights and recommendations

This skill gives you the clarity and confidence to make informed financial decisions aligned with your values and goals.
