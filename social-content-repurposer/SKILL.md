## Platform Best Practices

### Hook Formulas

**Instagram/TikTok:**
- Question: "Want to know the secret to...?"
- Bold claim: "This changed everything..."
- Relatable: "If you've ever struggled with..."
- Curiosity: "Nobody talks about this, but..."

**LinkedIn:**
- Insight: "After 5 years in [industry], I learned..."
- Story: "Last week, something unexpected happened..."
- Controversy: "Unpopular opinion: [take]..."
- Value: "Here are 5 things I wish I knew..."

**Email:**
- Personal: "I have a confession..."
- Exclusive: "You're the first to know..."
- Benefit: "This will save you [time/money/effort]..."
- Story: "Something interesting happened..."

## Content Calendar Strategy

### Posting Frequency Recommendations

- **Instagram**: 4-7x/week (Reels daily, feed posts 3-4x)
- **TikTok**: 1-3x/day for growth
- **YouTube Shorts**: 3-5x/week
- **LinkedIn**: 2-3x/week
- **Twitter/X**: 3-10x/day (mix of tweets and threads)
- **Newsletter**: 1x/week or bi-weekly

### Timing Strategy

**Same Content, Staggered Release:**
- Instagram: Mon 10am
- TikTok: Mon 6pm
- YouTube: Tues 12pm
- LinkedIn: Wed 9am
- Newsletter: Fri 8am

This prevents audience overlap fatigue while maximizing reach.
