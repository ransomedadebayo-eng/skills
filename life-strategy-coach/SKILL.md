# Life Strategy & Decision Coach

## Overview

This skill helps you evaluate life decisions—from new projects to habit changes to career moves—against your core values, purpose statement, and long-term vision.

**Keywords**: life decisions, values alignment, purpose-driven, life strategy, decision-making, priorities, goals alignment, life planning

## When to Use This Skill

- Evaluate new opportunities
- Decide what to stop doing
- Assess habit changes
- Make career decisions
- Prioritize competing goals
- Check purpose alignment
