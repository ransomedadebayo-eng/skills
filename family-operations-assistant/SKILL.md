## Home Systems & SOPs

### Weekly Cleaning Routine
```
WEEKLY CLEANING SOP
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DAILY (10 min)
□ Make beds
□ Dishes (load/unload dishwasher)
□ Wipe kitchen counters
□ 5-min tidy (pick up toys/clutter)

MONDAY: Laundry Day
□ Wash, dry, fold, put away

TUESDAY: Bathrooms
□ Clean toilets, sinks, mirrors
□ Restock supplies

WEDNESDAY: Floors
□ Vacuum main areas
□ Mop kitchen/bathrooms

THURSDAY: Dusting
□ Dust surfaces
□ Change bed sheets

FRIDAY: Catch-up/Prep
□ Grocery shopping
□ Meal prep for weekend
□ Tidy for weekend

SATURDAY: Deep clean (rotating)
Week 1: Kitchen (fridge, oven)
Week 2: Bathrooms (grout, fixtures)
Week 3: Bedrooms (closets, under beds)
Week 4: Living areas (windows, baseboards)

SUNDAY: Rest / Minimal
□ Plan week ahead
□ Light tidy
```

### Meal Planning System
```
MEAL PLANNING SOP
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

EVERY SUNDAY (30 min)

STEP 1: Plan meals (15 min)
- Check calendar (busy nights?)
- Choose 5 dinners
- Plan 2 "easy nights" (leftovers, takeout)

STEP 2: Make grocery list (10 min)
- Check pantry/fridge
- List ingredients needed
- Add household items

STEP 3: Shop (5 min to order)
- Use grocery delivery/pickup
- Schedule delivery for Monday

WEEKDAY ROUTINE

Easy Meals for Busy Nights:
- Slow cooker meals (prep morning)
- Sheet pan dinners (25 min)
- Instant Pot meals (30 min)
- Build-your-own (tacos, bowls)

Batch Cooking:
- Sunday: Prep 2-3 proteins
- Cook grains/pasta in bulk
- Chop veggies for week
```
