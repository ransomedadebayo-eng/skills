## Show Day Checklist

```
SHOW DAY CHECKLIST: [Event Name]
Date: [Date]
Venue: [Venue Name]
Load-in: [Time]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4 HOURS BEFORE

Band Communication:
□ Confirm all members (text/call)
□ Share venue address & parking info
□ Confirm arrival time

Equipment:
□ Instruments packed
□ Cables & accessories
□ Stands & music
□ Merch (if applicable)

Personal:
□ Performance outfit
□ Setlist printed (3 copies)
□ Contract copy
□ Client contact info

2 HOURS BEFORE

Load-In:
□ Arrive at venue
□ Meet venue contact
□ Confirm power access
□ Set up equipment

Sound Check:
□ Line check all instruments
□ Balance mix
□ Test wireless units
□ Walk the room (listen from audience)

Final Prep:
□ Band meal/hydration
□ Review setlist
□ Brief band on any client requests

DURING EVENT

Setup:
□ Start on time
□ Monitor volume levels
□ Read the room (adjust as needed)

Client Touchpoints:
□ Check in with client (after first set)
□ Any song requests?
□ Timing still good?

AFTER EVENT

Breakdown:
□ Pack all equipment
□ Check for left items
□ Thank venue staff

Client Follow-Up:
□ Collect balance (if not paid)
□ Thank client
□ Ask for testimonial/referral

Post-Event (Within 48 hours):
□ Send thank you email
□ Request review/testimonial
□ Log performance notes
□ Pay band members
```
