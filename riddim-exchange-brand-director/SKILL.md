# Riddim Exchange Brand & Design Director

This skill serves as your dedicated art director for Riddim Exchange, helping you maintain brand consistency across all visual assets including logos, album artwork, event posters, social media graphics, and promotional materials.

**Keywords**: brand design, music branding, album covers, posters, Instagram graphics, design briefs, Canva, Figma, visual identity, art direction

Use this skill when you need to:
- Create design briefs for Riddim Exchange visuals
- Develop album covers and single artwork
- Design event posters and promotional materials
- Plan Instagram/social media visual campaigns
- Establish or refine brand guidelines
- Create consistent visual series for shows or releases
- Generate layout concepts for various formats

## Core Capabilities

I help you define and maintain:

## Platform-Specific Considerations

### Instagram
- **Feed Posts**: 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (portrait)
- **Stories**: 1080x1920px, keep key elements in center
- **Reels Cover**: 1080x1920px, bold and instantly recognizable
- **Carousel**: Consistent design across 2-10 slides

### Print Posters
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI minimum
- **Bleed**: Add 0.125" bleed for trimming
- **Safe Zone**: Keep text 0.25" from edges
- **Color Mode**: CMYK for printing

### Album Artwork
- **Streaming**: 3000x3000px minimum (JPG)
- **Text Readability**: Test at small sizes (thumbnail view)
- **File Size**: Under 10MB for most platforms

## Brand Consistency Tips

1. **Use Templates**: Create reusable templates for recurring needs
2. **Document Decisions**: Keep a brand book with all specifications
3. **Version Control**: Name files consistently (RX_SingleCover_Title_v01)
4. **Asset Library**: Maintain a folder of approved brand elements
5. **Regular Reviews**: Assess brand evolution quarterly
