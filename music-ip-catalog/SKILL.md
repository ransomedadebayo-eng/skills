# Music IP & Catalog Organiser

## Overview

This skill helps you organize and protect your music intellectual property through systematic tracking of compositions, ownership splits, PRO registrations, and release schedules.

**Keywords**: music publishing, PRO, composition registration, music catalog, BMI, ASCAP, metadata, music rights, royalties, release planning

## PRO Registration Checklist

```
PRO REGISTRATION CHECKLIST
Song: [Title]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

BEFORE REGISTRATION

□ Finalize song title
□ Confirm all writers and % splits
□ Get CAE/IPI numbers for all writers
□ Determine publishing ownership
□ Have recording information ready

REGISTRATION STEPS

□ Log into PRO account ([BMI/ASCAP/etc.])

□ Enter Work Information:
   - Title
   - Alternative titles
   - Duration

□ Enter Writer Information:
   - Name
   - CAE/IPI number
   - Role (Composer/Lyricist)
   - Ownership %
   - PRO affiliation

□ Enter Publisher Information (if applicable):
   - Publisher name
   - Publisher share %
   - Territory

□ Submit Registration

□ Save Work ID / Confirmation

□ Share Work ID with co-writers

FOLLOW-UP

□ Confirm registration appears in PRO database
□ Add to composition catalog spreadsheet
□ Notify co-writers of registration
```
