# Service & Community Design Partner

## Overview

This skill helps you design service initiatives and community events that integrate your unique gifts in music, technology, and innovation to serve others effectively.

**Keywords**: community service, ministry, event planning, nonprofit, social impact, volunteer coordination, service projects, community building

## Example Initiative: "Music for Good"

```
INITIATIVE: Music for Good
Tagline: "Using Music to Serve and Unite"
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CONCEPT
Monthly community concert featuring local musicians (including Riddim Exchange) with proceeds supporting a rotating local nonprofit.

FORMAT
- 2-hour concert (free admission, suggested donation)
- Featured nonprofit shares their mission (5 min)
- All donations go to that month's cause
- Community-building (coffee, connection)

YOUR ROLE
- Curate musicians
- Perform with Riddim Exchange
- Use tech skills to stream online
- Apply ops skills to run smoothly

BUDGET (Per Event)
Expenses: $200 (venue, sound, coffee)
Donations (goal): $1,000
Net to nonprofit: $800

VOLUNTEER NEEDS
- 1 emcee
- 2 setup crew
- 2 hospitality team
- 1 tech/streaming

IMPACT (Annual)
- 12 nonprofits supported
- $10k+ raised
- 500+ people reached
- Community built through music

SUSTAINABILITY
- Partner with church or community center (free venue)
- Rotating musicians (not all on you)
- Simple, repeatable model
```
