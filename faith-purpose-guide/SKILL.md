# Faith & Purpose Reflection Guide

## Overview

This skill provides structured reflection prompts and decision frameworks to help you process life choices through the lens of faith, purpose, and calling.

**Keywords**: faith integration, purpose-driven living, spiritual reflection, calling, values alignment, decision-making, Christian living, stewardship

```

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[Honest assessment of the situation]

How does this connect to my faith?
[Prayer, Scripture, spiritual disciplines]

```

```
Rating: [1-5]

□ Does this use the gifts God gave me?

CHARACTER IMPACT
□ Does this grow me spiritually?
□ Does this require faith/dependence on God?
□ Does this build Christ-like character?
Rating: [1-5]

□ Is this a wise use of time/money/energy?
□ Does this multiply my talents or bury them?
□ Does this build eternal value?
Rating: [1-5]

TOTAL SCORE: XX/25

DISCERNMENT QUESTION:
Is this opportunity a divine appointment or a distraction?
```

## Purpose Statement Development

```
CRAFTING YOUR PURPOSE STATEMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

REFLECTION QUESTIONS

1. GIFTS: What has God uniquely gifted you to do?
[Natural talents, acquired skills, spiritual gifts]

2. PASSION: What breaks your heart or lights you up?
[Burdens, joys, what you can't stop thinking about]

3. EXPERIENCE: What unique story has shaped you?
[Hardships, victories, unusual path]

4. IMPACT: What change do you want to see in the world?
[The difference you want to make]

5. LEGACY: What do you want to be remembered for?
[How you want others to describe your life]

PURPOSE STATEMENT FORMULA:

I exist to [VERB - what you do]
for [WHO - who you serve]
so that [IMPACT - the change you create]
to [ULTIMATE WHY - glory to God / kingdom impact].

EXAMPLE:
"I exist to bridge operations and creativity for leaders and creatives so that they can maximize their impact without sacrificing their souls, ultimately pointing people to a God who redeems all of life."
```
