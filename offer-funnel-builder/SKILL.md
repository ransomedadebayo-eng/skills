## Offer Positioning Framework

### The 4 Ps of Offer Design

**1. PROMISE (Outcome)**
What will the customer achieve?
- Bass Coaching: "Play confidently in any musical situation"
- Band Booking: "Unforgettable event that your guests rave about"
- Consulting: "Streamlined operations that save 20% of your time"

**2. PROBLEM (Pain Point)**
What pain are you solving?
- Bass Coaching: "Stuck in a rut, not progressing"
- Band Booking: "Generic music that doesn't energize your event"
- Consulting: "Inefficient processes costing time and money"

**3. PROOF (Credibility)**
Why should they trust you?
- Credentials (Apple OPM experience)
- Results (student/client testimonials)
- Social proof (years of experience, performances)

**4. PRICE (Investment)**
What does it cost and what's included?
- Clear pricing tiers
- Payment plans
- Value stack (show total value vs. price)

## Funnel Metrics to Track

**Awareness Stage:**
- Traffic to landing page
- Cost per click (if running ads)
- Social media reach

**Interest Stage:**
- Lead magnet conversion rate (aim for 20-40%)
- Email open rates (aim for 20-30%)
- Email click-through rates (aim for 2-5%)

**Consideration Stage:**
- Discovery call booking rate
- Show-up rate for calls
- Email-to-call conversion

**Conversion Stage:**
- Call-to-customer conversion (aim for 20-50%)
- Average order value
- Payment plan vs. full-pay ratio

**Retention Stage:**
- Customer lifetime value
- Referral rate
- Upsell/cross-sell rate
