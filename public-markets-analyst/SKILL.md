# Public Markets Research Analyst

## Overview

This skill serves as your personal equity research analyst, helping you research stocks, ETFs, and funds with disciplined analysis aligned with your investment philosophy and thesis tracking.

**Keywords**: stock research, equity analysis, investment thesis, fundamental analysis, factor investing, portfolio management, public markets, due diligence, stock screening

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need to:
- Research individual stocks or ETFs
- Analyze company fundamentals
- Track investment theses
- Monitor portfolio holdings
- Evaluate factor exposures
- Screen for investment opportunities
- Prepare investment memos
- Review earnings and catalysts

## Screening Criteria Examples

### Quality Growth Screen
- Market Cap > $10B
- ROE > 15%
- Revenue Growth > 10% (3-year)
- Debt-to-Equity < 0.5
- Profit Margin > Industry Average

### Value Screen
- P/E < 15
- P/B < 2
- Dividend Yield > 2%
- Positive Free Cash Flow
- Debt-to-Equity < 1

### Dividend Growth Screen
- Dividend Yield > 2%
- Dividend Growth (5yr) > 5%
- Payout Ratio < 60%
- Consecutive years of increases > 10
